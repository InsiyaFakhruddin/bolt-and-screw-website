Bolt & Screw Trading is a Karachi-based industrial fastener supplier serving construction, manufacturing, textile, and energy sectors across Pakistan. We are committed to quality, specification accuracy, and fast delivery.

Our Story

KARACHI'S TRUSTED FASTENER SUPPLIER

Located at Fazal Chamber on Murad Khan Road — one of Karachi's key industrial trading hubs — Bolt & Screw Trading has served contractors, fabricators, and manufacturers with the fasteners that keep projects moving.

From a single hex bolt to a bulk consignment of stainless anchor studs, we stock what industry needs. Our team understands specifications — DIN, ANSI, ISO — and helps customers select the right grade and material for every application.

We believe in transparent pricing, accurate specifications, and building long-term relationships with our clients. Whether you're a small workshop or a large-scale contractor, we give everyone the same attention.

🎯 Specification Accuracy

We stock to DIN, ANSI, and ISO standards. Every product is clearly labeled with grade, material, and finish — no guesswork.

📦 Stock Availability

Wide range kept in ready stock. Most orders can be fulfilled same-day or next-day across Karachi.

💬 Expert Advice

Not sure which fastener to use? Our team helps you specify the correct grade and size for your exact application.

🤝 Fair Pricing

Competitive rates for both retail and wholesale. Bulk pricing available for contractors and regular clients.
