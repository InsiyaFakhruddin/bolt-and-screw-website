OUR STORY

FROM ONE SHOP TO
FULL CATALOG

Bolt & Screw Trading started with a simple idea — make it easier for contractors and engineers in Karachi to source the exact hardware they need, without the runaround. We set up shop at Fazal Chamber, Murad Khan Road, and built our inventory around what local industry actually needed.

Over the years, our catalog grew from basic hex bolts and nuts to a comprehensive range covering anchors, specialty screws, threaded rods, and more. We work directly with manufacturers to ensure consistent quality and competitive pricing on every order.

Today we serve construction companies, textile mills, oil & gas contractors, and machine shops across Karachi and beyond. Every order, big or small, gets the same attention to accuracy and speed.
